Common Questions & Answers

ScanZu is a QR code Smart Doorbell system, that instantly lets you know who is at the door no matter where you are.

To get in touch with you, your visitors simply scan your code with any smartphone (they don't need any app), and you are instantly connected in real time.

ScanZu is designed to be compatible with any smartphone that can read QR codes. Your visitors don't need to download any App to get in touch with you using your ScanZu. They just need to scan your code with their phone to contact you.

To order a ScanZu QR kit, simply login to ScanZu website or app and order. We are also available on Amazon and Blinkit. Once your ScanZu QR kit arrives, you simply need to activate and finish the setup for you and the rest of your family. Other members dont need to setup the account and can simply be managed by primary member.
